Today, RK Hospitals Group has touched the lives of over 45 million patients from 121 countries. RK Hospitals is known not only as a medical institution but has emerged as an integrated healthcare provider in Asia, specializing also in consultancy, clinics, pharmacy, insurance and holistic therapy.
